Privacy Policy

Golf Slope Reader Pro does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app uses on-device sensors (accelerometer) to detect slope angles for the purpose of providing real-time golf insights. All sensor processing is done locally on your device.

Voice feedback is provided using the system’s built-in Text-to-Speech engine. Vibration feedback is triggered locally based on slope intensity.

This app does not connect to the internet and does not transmit any data to any server.

We take privacy seriously and ensure full compliance with Google Play policies, the GDPR, and other global data protection laws.

If you have any questions, you may contact us at: support@ajnyata.com
