Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application "Cat Identifier : Lynxia" (“we”, “our”, or “us”). It describes how we handle your data when you use the app to identify marine species.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect or store any personal information from users of this app. The app can be used without signing in or providing any personal identifiers.

2. Image Upload and Identification

To identify species in your images, your images may be temporarily uploaded to a secure server for analysis. These images are used solely for the purpose of identification and are automatically deleted immediately after processing. We do not retain, access, or share your images.

3. Security

All file transfers are encrypted and handled via secure HTTPS connections. Files are not accessed by any human and are never shared with third parties.

4. Analytics and Tracking

We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking services at this time.

We currently do not collect personal information or usage metrics. If this changes in a future version of the app, we will update this Privacy Policy and notify users as required.

6. Data Retention

Uploaded images are deleted automatically after processing. We do not store uploaded or processed images on our servers after the session ends.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: splopshq@proton.me
